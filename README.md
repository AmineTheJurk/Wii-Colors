# Wii-Colors
A Homebrew App That Shows Pretty Colors On Your Wii
#Controls
Click A To Switch Colors
#Files
the zip comes with 2 folders WADS and the folder apps
the wads folder has 2 wads. Colors - Forwarder.wad : a Forwarder a channel that automaticly launches the boot.dol from your SDCARD. Colors - Non-forwarder - Embedded Channel.wad : NOT A FORWARDER boots the boot.dol from the channel itself so no needing the homebrew app.
the apps folder is the homebrew app
